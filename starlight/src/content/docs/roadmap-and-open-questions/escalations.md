When the team's process cannot close a decision, the call escalates here. Each entry names the question, the alternatives, what the team has done to resolve it, and what Seb needs to provide to close it.

## E-001 — Watch-builder frame launch timing

**Question.** Do the two mandatory watch-builder frames (next-frame selector and promotion-readiness board) ship at v1 launch or in a v1.1 release two to four weeks later?

**Alternatives.** See [O-001](/roadmap-and-open-questions/options-to-decide/#o-001-watch-builder-frame-launch-timing). Three options: at v1, v1.1, or conditional.

**Why the team cannot close.** The team is genuinely split. One faction holds that the soul thread is the launch story and slipping the watch-builder frames is shipping the wrong v1. Another holds that the schema work needs to land first and coupling the watch-builder frames to the launch deadline slips both. A third position notes that until the C1a test confirms anyone wants to watch, the timing question is downstream of the existence question.

**What Seb needs to provide.** A call between the three options, possibly contingent: "at v1 if Packet 1 lands by [date], otherwise v1.1." A no-go date is acceptable; "I'll decide after C1a" is acceptable.

**Status.** Open.

## E-002 — Legal review timing

**Question.** When do we engage a lawyer on the CC0 + attested-acceptance mechanics?

**Context.** Not v1-blocking. Blocking before any external contributor. The longer the team waits, the higher the cost of a structural change to the acceptance model.

**What Seb needs to provide.** A target window: "before the first external contributor," "before v1.1 GA," or a fixed date. Plus, if available, a lawyer contact.

**Status.** Open.

## E-003 — C1a test scheduling

**Question.** When do we run the C1a test (three first-time visitors on the current prototype, looking for "watching agents work" vs. "screenshot gallery")?

**Context.** Cheapest test of the product thesis. Until it runs, every downstream decision is partially bet on an unverified premise.

**What Seb needs to provide.** A target week, a recruitment plan (three willing first-time visitors), and a willingness to act on a "screenshot gallery" result.

**Status.** Open.

## How escalation works

The team escalates only when:

1. The question has been council-debated and remains split.
2. The alternatives are concrete (the entry would otherwise live in [Options to decide](/roadmap-and-open-questions/options-to-decide/)).
3. The blocker is preventing v1-relevant work.

An entry that does not meet all three lives in [Open questions](/roadmap-and-open-questions/open-questions/).
